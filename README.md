# Sales Analysis Case Study

## Introduction:
A generic, simulated dataset which indicates the daily trading information for a large retail store has been provided. The data is only for one of the products sold at the store. 

## Objective:
To develop metrics and derive subsequent insights.
The metrics include;
- The sales price per unit
- The average unit sales price of the product
- The daily % gross profit
- The Price Elasticity of Demand during one of the promotional/special periods

Databricks
